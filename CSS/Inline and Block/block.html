Web page content refers to the text, images, videos, and other media that make up the information on a website. It's the material users interact with when they visit a webpage. This content can include anything from written articles and blog posts to product descriptions, images, videos, and even interactive elements like forms or games. Good web page content is well-structured, easy to read, and relevant to the user's needs, aiming to engage and inform them effectively.
